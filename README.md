## Motivation
The climate crisis is wreaking havoc on the world, and flights are the most carbon-intensive activity most individuals partake in. However, flights are a necessary tool in modern society. Remote companies, conference organizers, and event managers could make a real impact by optimizing their meeting locations to reduce their COâ‚‚ impact. 

## Limitations
- Actual routing isn't factored in (because routing data is expensive and proprietary), meaning that only direct flights are being calculated, whether or not the direct flight actually exists.
- Only flights are factored in at this time (no option for other modes of transporation)
